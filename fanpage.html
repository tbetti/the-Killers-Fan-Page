Bio

The Killers formed in Las Vegas in 2001. They gained international fame in 2003 after releasing their hit song "Mr. Brightside" in the UK. They also released their first album, Hot Fuss, the same year. Since then, they have released five more albums including their most recent, Imploding the Mirage, released in 2020.

  • Sam's Town, 2006
  • Day & Age, 2008
  • Battle & Born, 2012
